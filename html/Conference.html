At least one author of each paper must register for the conference. Each full (normal) registration is entitled to 1 paper. All fees are inclusive of registration, proceedings and the conference kit. Note that the fees are exclusive of bank transfer charges and participants MUST add on any addiitonal bank charges. Official receipt will be given during the conference. Papers not presented during the conference will not be considered for inclusion in IEEEXplore.

Registration and payment details will be emailed to authors after the notification of acceptance has been sent.

IICAIET2023

The IEEE Sabah subsection is organizing the 2023 IEEE International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Engineering and Technology (IICAIET2023), co-organized by the Artificial Intelligence Research Unit and Universiti Malaysia Sabah (UMS), which will be held at Kota Kinabalu, Malaysia from 12 to 14 September2023..
