ACT2024

The IEEE Sabah subsection is organizing the 2024 IEEE First International Conference on Intelligence in Engineering (ACT 2024), co-organized by the Artificial Intelligence Research Unit and Universiti Malaysia Sabah (UMS), which will be held at Kota Kinabalu, Malaysia from 12 to 14 September 2024.
