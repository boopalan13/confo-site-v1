Scope of the Conference

The conference aims to explore cutting-edge developments at the intersection of intelligence and engineering, fostering interdisciplinary discussions. Topics include AI applications in design, smart systems, sustainable engineering, and ethical considerations. Participants will engage in knowledge exchange, highlighting advancements that shape the future of intelligent engineering practices.

Vision And Mission of the Conference

Vision: Empowering the global engineering community through the synergy of intelligence, innovation, and collaboration, driving transformative advancements in the field.
Mission: To provide a dynamic platform for researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to share insights, exchange ideas, and propel the integration of intelligence into engineering practices. Fostering a community that pioneers ethical, sustainable, and intelligent solutions to address the challenges of a rapidly evolving technological landscape.
